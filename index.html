Responsible use of Social media

The responsible use of social media is crucial in today's interconnected world. Users should prioritize authencity, mindful sharing, and respectful engagement to foster a positive online environment. Additionally, being vigilant against mis-information and understanding the impact of one;s digital footprint are essential for responsible digital citizenship
